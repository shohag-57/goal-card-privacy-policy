Privacy Policy — Goal Card App

Last updated: October 17, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Goal Card App ("we," "our," or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about our data practices.

Information Collection and Storage

Local Storage Only

Goal Card App operates entirely on your device. All your data, including:

  • Your goals and goal cards
  • Reminder settings
  • App preferences and settings
  • Background images you upload
  • Any other personal information entered into the app

is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers or cloud services.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not store your data on external servers
  • We do not share your data with third parties (except as noted below for advertising)

Permissions

Notifications

Purpose: To send you reminders about your goals at times you specify.
Usage: All notification processing happens locally on your device.

Storage / Photo Library

Purpose: To allow you to upload custom background images.
Usage: Images are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information to provide personalized advertising. This includes:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • App usage data for ad performance

Important: This advertising data is collected by Google, not us. We do not have access to this data. For more information:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings (Android/iOS).

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend using device encryption, screen locks, and regular backups.

Data Retention and Deletion

Automatic Deletion

If you uninstall the Goal Card App, all your data will be permanently deleted from your device. We have no way to recover this data as we do not store it on our servers.

Manual Deletion

You can delete individual goals at any time within the app. Once deleted, the data cannot be recovered.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect user data, the app can be used by individuals of all ages under parental supervision.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Backup Recommendation

Since we store all data locally on your device, we strongly recommend:

  • Not uninstalling the app if you want to keep your goals
  • Using your device's backup features (iCloud for iOS, Google Backup for Android)
  • Taking screenshots of important goals if needed

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your data, you have complete control over it. No data is shared with us or third parties (except AdMob as described). You can delete the app and all associated data at any time.
